How Revenue-Share is Calculated

We track attributed revenue — sales, leads, or transactions that came directly from automated workflows. This might include: form submissions that converted, scheduled calls booked via automation, customers routed through automation, etc.

Each month, we report actual attribution, calculate your %, and send you an invoice. Full transparency. You can verify the numbers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why revenue-share instead of a fixed monthly fee?

Because we believe in alignment. We take on the risk of your success. Your growth is our growth. If automation doesn't drive revenue, we adjust the partnership.

What counts as "attributed revenue"?

Revenue directly tied to automated workflows. Examples: customers from a form submission, calls booked via calendar automation, orders placed by users routed through automation. We define attribution clearly in every contract.

Can I switch to a fixed monthly fee instead?

Yes. If you prefer a predictable cost, we offer hybrid models: smaller setup fee + lower monthly flat rate (e.g., $1,500/month). Let's discuss what works for you.

What happens if the automation doesn't increase revenue?

We optimize for free during the first 90 days. No performance = we iterate, refine, or adjust terms. We're invested in your success.

Is there a minimum contract term?

Yes. Typically 12 months to give the automation time to generate ROI. We can discuss shorter terms or exit clauses if needed.

Do you work with startups that haven't launched yet?

Absolutely. We've worked with pre-launch founders building MVP automations. Pricing adjusts for early-stage: lower setup fee, tiered revenue-share as you scale.
