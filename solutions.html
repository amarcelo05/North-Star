Automation Solutions

From intake to monitoring — we handle every step of your workflow. Discover which solutions fit your business.

Core Workflow: Intake → Normalize → Schedule → Monitor

1. Intake

Collect customer data automatically.

  • Form-to-database pipelines
  • Email-triggered workflows
  • Lead capture & qualification
  • Client onboarding forms

Benefit: No more manual data entry. Leads flow instantly into your system.

2. Normalize

Clean and organize raw data.

  • Deduplicate customer records
  • Auto-format phone/email
  • Tag and categorize leads
  • Merge partial records

Benefit: Consistent, clean data ready for action. No garbage in, garbage out.

3. Schedule

Sync calendars and book automatically.

  • Google Calendar integration
  • Meeting reminder emails
  • Booking confirmation
  • Automatic reschedule on conflict

Benefit: Zero double-bookings. Clients confirm in advance. Fewer no-shows.

4. Monitor

Track automation health & results.

  • Workflow success/failure logs
  • Time-saved metrics
  • Monthly impact reports
  • Alert on errors

Benefit: Prove ROI. Identify bottlenecks. Optimize continuously.

Email Integration

Automate email-triggered actions.

  • Parse inbound emails
  • Route to assignees
  • Send templated replies
  • Log to database

Benefit: Faster response times. No lost messages.

Custom Workflows

Chain solutions together.

  • Multi-step automation
  • Conditional logic
  • Third-party integrations
  • Scalable to your needs

Benefit: Automate the whole funnel, not just one task.

Technology Stack

We use industry-standard tools to ensure reliability, security, and scalability:

  • Forms & Intake: Zapier, Make, Google Forms, Typeform
  • Database: Airtable, Supabase, Google Sheets
  • Calendar & Email: Google Calendar API, Zapier Email
  • Automation: Make, Zapier, custom APIs
  • Payments (future): Stripe

All integrations are secure, tested, and designed to scale with your business.
